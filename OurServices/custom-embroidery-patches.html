Exploring Our Comprehensive CUSTOM PATCHES

At Sabion Embroidery, we take pride in delivering high-quality custom patches that meet your specific requirements. Our custom patches are meticulously crafted using top-grade materials and advanced embroidery techniques to ensure durability and vibrant detail. Whether you need patches for uniforms, promotional items, or personal projects, we offer a wide range of options including embroidered, woven, and PVC patches. Each patch is tailored to your design, and we handle everything from digitizing your artwork to the physical delivery of the final product.

SUBLIMATION PATCH

Our sublimation patch service offers a unique and vibrant alternative to traditional embroidery. Using cutting-edge sublimation technology, we create patches with stunning, full-color designs that are perfect for capturing intricate details and gradients. These patches are ideal for logos, photographs, and complex artwork, as the sublimation process embeds the ink directly into the fabric, ensuring long-lasting color and durability. Whether for uniforms, sports teams, or promotional items, our sublimation patches provide a smooth, lightweight finish that won’t fade or peel. We handle the entire process, from design to physical delivery, ensuring you receive high-quality patches that exceed your expectations.

WOVEN PATCH

Our woven patch service offers a sophisticated and detailed option for your custom patch needs. Using high-density weaving techniques, we create patches with sharp, clean lines and intricate details that are perfect for intricate logos, text, and fine artwork. Unlike embroidered patches, woven patches have a smooth surface and can capture small details with exceptional clarity. Ideal for uniforms, corporate branding, and promotional items, our woven patches are durable and retain their vibrant colors over time. We manage the entire production process, from precise digitizing to careful weaving, and deliver the finished patches right to your doorstep, ensuring top-notch quality and satisfaction.

EMBROIDERY PATCH

Our embroidery patch service specializes in crafting high-quality patches with rich textures and vibrant colors. Using advanced embroidery machines and premium threads, we create patches that add a classic and professional touch to any garment. Perfect for uniforms, team apparel, and promotional items, our embroidered patches can be customized to match your exact design specifications, including intricate details and vibrant colors. Each patch is durable, withstanding regular wear and washing, ensuring they look great for years. From initial design to final delivery, we handle the entire process, providing you with beautifully embroidered patches that stand out and make a lasting impression.

PVC PATCH

Our PVC patch service offers durable, flexible, and weather-resistant patches perfect for high-impact applications. Made from premium quality polyvinyl chloride (PVC), these patches are ideal for outdoor gear, tactical uniforms, sports equipment, and promotional items. PVC patches are known for their vibrant colors and 3D layered designs, allowing for intricate details and a modern look that stands out. Whether you need a simple logo or a detailed design, our PVC patches are custom-made to meet your specifications. We handle everything from design creation to production and physical delivery, ensuring you receive high-quality PVC patches that are both functional and visually striking.

LEATHER PATCH

Our leather patch service provides a touch of elegance and sophistication to your custom patches. Crafted from high-quality genuine or faux leather, these patches are perfect for adding a premium feel to your products, including jackets, bags, hats, and more. Leather patches can be embossed, debossed, or printed to match your design specifications, offering a timeless and durable option for branding or personalization. Each patch is meticulously crafted to ensure precise detailing and a refined finish. From concept to final delivery, we manage the entire process, ensuring you receive exquisite leather patches that elevate the aesthetic of your items.
